our promise

Our warranty, in plain English.

If we fixed it and it acts up, you call us. We come back. That's the whole idea. Here are the specifics, written like a human wrote them.

90 days

Labor warranty on every repair and installation we perform.

Parts coverage

Manufacturer parts warranty continues after the labor period ends.

What's covered

90-day labor warranty

If a part we installed fails within 90 days of service, we come back and fix it at no additional labor cost. Just call us.

Parts after 90 days

After the 90-day labor period ends, parts remain covered under their manufacturer warranty. A $49.95 service fee applies for the trip and diagnosis — that's our cost, not yours.

$200 labor cap on opener & spring replacements

Out of warranty? Even then, labor for replacing an opener or springs is capped at $200 — no matter how long it takes. You'll never get a surprise four-figure labor bill from us.

What's not covered

Damage from misuse, accidents, weather events, attempted self-repair, or normal wear-and-tear on consumables (weather stripping, batteries) isn't covered. We'll always tell you straight if a problem falls in that bucket.

How to use the warranty

Just call us at (916) 435-6320. Tell us when we did the work and what's happening. We'll book a follow-up — usually same week.

Something we fixed acting up?

Call us. That's literally what the warranty is for.
