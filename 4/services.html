how a service call works

Four simple steps. No surprises.

1

You call

Real human picks up. We ask what's happening with your door and book a window that works for you — usually same-day.

2

We show up

On time, in a stocked truck. We diagnose in front of you and give you a flat, written quote before any work starts.

3

We fix it

The vast majority of jobs are done in one visit. Both springs always replaced. Free lube + tune included.

4

We stand behind it

90-day labor warranty. If anything we touched gives you trouble, you call us, we come back — no hassle.
