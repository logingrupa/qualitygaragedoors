our story

The neighborhood garage door people.

A family-run Sacramento shop. Fixing doors, replacing springs, and being a phone call away — for over 20 years now.

how we got here

Started small. Stayed small. On purpose.

We've been doing this for over 20 years and we like it the way it is: a small crew, a few well-stocked trucks, and a phone that gets answered. We're not trying to be the biggest garage door company in Sacramento. We just try to be the one you trust.

Tim has been swinging tools longer than most of us have been driving. The crew learned from him — same standards, same friendly approach, same "fix it like it's our mom's house" mindset.

If you've called us before, you know. If this is your first time — welcome. We'll take good care of your door.

what we believe

Four house rules we don't break.

Both springs, every time.

If one snapped, the other one's running on borrowed time. We replace both on every spring repair so you don't pay for a second visit two weeks from now.

Free lube + tune. Always.

Every repair gets a free lube, tune, and full safety inspection — normally a $129 service. It's just baked in. No upsell.

The price we say is the price.

No mystery fees, no "while we're here" pressure. We quote, you decide, we work. That's the whole transaction.

We answer the phone.

Mon–Sat 7AM–7PM, real human, no menu tree. After hours? 24-hour emergency line for the actually-stuck-and-can't-leave situations.
