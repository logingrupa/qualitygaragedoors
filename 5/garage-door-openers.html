A / REPAIR

Opener repair (any brand).

If your existing opener is repairable, we'd rather repair it than sell you a new one. We service every common residential brand. The most frequent failures are listed below.

01GEAR & SPROCKET
Worn nylon gear is a common failure. Replaced with hardened sets.
02CIRCUIT BOARD
Logic board faults after power surges. Often replaceable in-place.
03SAFETY EYES
Photo-eye misalignment is the most-overlooked cause of door reversals.
04TROLLEY / RACK
Trolley wear, broken rack teeth, slipped chain — all repairable.
05LIMIT & FORCE
Limit switch and force-setting recalibration to factory tolerance.
06REMOTES
Remote programming, wall-control replacement, MyQ pairing.
B / NEW INSTALL

New Liftmaster, fitted today.

Liftmaster belt-drive openers are the quietest residential units on the market. The belt itself is steel-reinforced rubber rather than chain — there is no metal-on-metal noise, and the unit is dramatically quieter against bedrooms above the garage.

Same-day installation is normal for our crew: we call ahead, we arrive with a stocked truck, and the install typically completes in under two hours including setup of remotes, wall control, and safety eye alignment.
