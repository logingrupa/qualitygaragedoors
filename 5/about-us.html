01 / PRACTICE

What we do, and how.

Quality Garage Doors is a Sacramento residential garage door practice. We repair springs, cables, rollers, and tracks; we install Liftmaster openers; and we fit new steel, carriage-house, and custom wood doors throughout Sacramento, Placer, and El Dorado counties.

Our crew is small on purpose. Tim — the lead technician most of our customers meet — has been working on doors for over 20 years. The tools and parts on each truck are the ones we actually use, every day. That's why we can usually finish the job on the first visit.

We do not pressure-sell. We do not invent extras. We replace both torsion springs as standard, include a free lube and tune with every repair, and use heavy-duty hardware. Where the work needs to be done quickly, it is. Where it needs to be done carefully, it is.
