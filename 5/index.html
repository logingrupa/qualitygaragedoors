02 APPROACH

Honest work,
line by line.

01BOTH SPRINGSREPLACED
When one torsion spring fails, the other is at the end of its lifespan too. We replace both as standard. Replacing only the broken spring puts excess load on the surviving one and shortens its life.
02FREE LUBE + TUNE$129 VALUE
Every repair includes a complimentary lube and tune of door and opener. Sold standalone at $129. Included so the system runs cleanly after we leave.
03HEAVY‑DUTY HARDWARESTANDARD
Cables, rollers, hinges, drums, brackets — we use heavy-duty parts. The price difference is small. The lifespan difference is not.
04LIFTMASTER ONLYOPENERS
We install one opener brand: Liftmaster belt-drive. Quietest on the market, parts are available, and the warranty pathway is clean. Stocked on every truck.
05FREE HAUL‑AWAYNEW DOORS
Replacement installs include disassembly and removal of the old door, hardware, and tracks at no extra cost.
