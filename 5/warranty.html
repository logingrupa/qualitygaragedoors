A / TERMS

What's covered, line by line.

01LABOR WARRANTY· 90 DAYS
If a part we installed fails within 90 days of service, we return and fix it at no additional labor cost. Just call.
02PARTS — AFTER 90 DAYS· MANUFACTURER
After the labor period, parts remain covered under their manufacturer warranty. A $49.95 service fee applies for the trip and diagnosis.
03LABOR CAP· $200 MAX
Out-of-warranty labor for opener or spring replacement is capped at $200, so the maximum labor exposure is known up front.
04WARRANTY HOURS
Warranty service is scheduled Mon–Fri, 9AM–5PM. Outside those hours, our standard same-day and 24-hour emergency service is always available.
05RESIDENTIAL ONLY
We service residential garage doors. Commercial roll-up doors and industrial systems are not in our practice.
06EXCLUSIONS
Damage from misuse, accidents, weather, attempted self-repair, and normal wear on consumables (e.g. weatherstripping) is not covered.
B / FINE PRINT

Honest fine print.

This warranty applies to parts and labor performed by Quality Garage Doors. It does not cover damage caused by misuse, accidents, weather events, attempted self-repair, or normal wear on consumables.

Manufacturer warranties for installed parts (springs, opener motors, panels, etc.) are passed through to the customer and are governed by their own terms; we will help you initiate a warranty claim with the manufacturer if a covered part fails.
