Steel Doors

The workhorse. Long-life steel doors handle Sacramento weather, resist denting, and come in a wide range of panel styles and finishes. Every steel install includes new spring sets sized to the door, new tracks where needed, and heavy-duty hardware throughout.

Available in single-layer, double-layer with insulation, or triple-layer 2-inch steel-backed insulated panels for the quietest, warmest, and most secure option.

Carriage-House Doors

Carriage doors swing the look without the swing — overhead operation in a carriage-style face. Composite overlays on steel cores, real-wood looks without the maintenance, and your pick of hardware accents.

If you want the look in real timber, see custom wood below.

Custom Wood Doors

For homeowners who want the real thing. Cedar, redwood, mahogany, hemlock — built to your exact opening size and stained to match the trim. Wood doors require periodic finishing; we'll walk you through what that costs over time so the decision's made with full information.

Insulation matters A 2-inch steel-backed insulated door drops noise, cuts garage temperature swings, and adds rigidity that extends the life of every other component. Worth the spec on any attached garage.
