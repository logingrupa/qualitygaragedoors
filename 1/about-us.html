The Crew Behind The Wrenches.

Quality Garage Doors is a locally owned Sacramento operation. Twenty-plus years in the trade, on the same trucks, with the same hardware standards. We pick up the phone. We show up the day we say we will.

Our story

Started In Garages. Stayed In Garages.

We're a family-run garage door company serving Sacramento, Placer, and El Dorado Counties for over 20 years. We started with one truck and a stocked spring rack. Today the trucks are bigger and the rack is fuller — but the work hasn't changed.

Every job runs through the same rules: replace both springs, never just the broken one. Use heavy-duty hardware, never the bargain bin. Throw in the lube & tune for free. Show up when we said we would.

That's the whole pitch. No franchise. No call-center scripts. Just Tim and the crew, building the kind of trade reputation Sacramento contractors used to be famous for.

Operating standards

Five Rules. No Exceptions.

01

Both Springs. Always.

Springs wear at the same rate. Replacing one puts the new one under load it wasn't sized for. We replace the pair.

02

Heavy-Duty Hardware Only

Cables, rollers, hinges, brackets — commercial-grade defaults. No upgrades to "find."

03

Free Lube + Tune With Every Repair

04
