No legal Russian-doll wording. Here's exactly what's covered, for how long, and what you owe if something goes sideways.

SECTION 02 / TERMS
What's covered

Read It Before You Sign.

01

90-Day Labor Warranty

If a part we installed fails within 90 days of service, we return and fix it at no additional labor cost. Just call.

02

Parts Coverage After 90 Days

Parts remain covered under their original manufacturer terms (Liftmaster, springs, hardware). The trip + diagnosis is $49.95 to come out.

03

Out-Of-Warranty Labor Cap

Opener and spring replacement labor on out-of-warranty calls is capped at $200. We tell you up front before any work starts.

04

What's Excluded

Damage from misuse, accidents, weather events, attempted self-repair, or normal wear on consumables (weatherstripping, lube, batteries) is not covered. We'll always be straight with you about which bucket your call falls in.

05

How To Make A Claim

Call (916) 435-6320 and tell us your address and the date of original service. No paperwork hunt — we keep our own records.
