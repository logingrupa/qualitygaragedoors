Our story

Built by craftsmen, run by neighbors.

Quality Garage Doors is a small, locally owned Sacramento outfit. Tim and the crew have spent over twenty years on residential garage doors across the region — from first-time spring repairs in midtown bungalows to custom carriage-house installs on half-million-dollar Folsom homes.

We don't run a call center. The phone rings to a person. The truck shows up with the parts. Both springs get replaced — not just the broken one — because that's how a balanced, long-lasting door is supposed to work.

What you can expect

  • An honest, written quote at the truck — no high-pressure pitch.
  • Heavy-duty parts and Liftmaster openers carried on every service call.
  • Both torsion springs replaced on every spring repair.
  • Free lube + tune + safety inspection (normally $129) included with every repair.
  • 90-day labor warranty on every job, plus manufacturer parts coverage.
  • Free haul-away of your old door on every new installation.

What we stand by

A short list of standards we don't bend on.

  1. 01

    Lowest rates in town

    Transparent, upfront pricing — written at the truck before any work begins. No surprise add-ons, no fine-print fees.

  2. 02

    Both springs replaced — always

    When one torsion spring breaks, the other is at end of life. We replace both, every time, so the door stays balanced and the opener doesn't take the strain.

  3. 03

    Free lube + tune + inspection

    Every repair includes a complimentary lube, tune and safety inspection — normally a $129 service on its own.

  4. 04

    Liftmaster only

    We install Liftmaster openers exclusively. They're the most reliable belt-drive units on the market, and our crew knows them inside and out.

  5. 05

    Free haul-away

    On every new door installation, we remove your old door and hardware at no charge. The driveway is clean when we leave.

  6. 06

    Licensed, bonded, insured

    CA Contractor License #1042651. BBB A+ rated. Fully bonded and insured for residential work across Sacramento, Placer and El Dorado Counties.
