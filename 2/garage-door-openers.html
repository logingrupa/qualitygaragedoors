Why Liftmaster

The only opener brand we install.

Liftmaster has been the residential opener of choice for serious installers for decades — quiet belt-drive operation, durable DC motors, and a smart-home ecosystem (MyQ) that actually works. We carry units on every truck for same-day install.

Belt-drive replacements

Belt-drive Liftmaster openers are dramatically quieter than older chain-drive units — a real difference if your garage is below or beside a bedroom. Soft start/stop motion is gentler on the door and the springs.

Smart-home integration

Most current Liftmaster units support Wi-Fi and the MyQ app — open and close the door from your phone, get alerts when it's left open, integrate with home automation systems.

Battery backup

Most Sacramento power outages happen at the worst possible moment. A Liftmaster opener with battery backup keeps the door working through outages and meets California Senate Bill 969 requirements for new opener installs.

Opener repair on every brand

We repair more than we replace. Most opener faults trace to one of a few common parts — and we carry them all on the truck.

  • Gear assemblies and motor sprockets — the most common point of failure on older units.
  • Logic boards / circuit boards — sun damage, surge damage, age.
  • Safety-eye photo sensors — knocked out of alignment or damaged.
  • Remote controls and keypads — re-pairing, replacement, multi-button setup.
  • Trolleys, rails, and drive belts — full assembly replacement.
  • Capacitors and limit switches on legacy AC openers.
