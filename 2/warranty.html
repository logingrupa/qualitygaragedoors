Our warranty, plainly written.

Every repair and installation we do is backed by a 90-day labor warranty. Manufacturer parts coverage continues from there. The fine print, in plain language.

The terms

What's covered, and for how long.

  1. 01

    90-day labor warranty

    Every repair and installation we perform is covered by a 90-day labor warranty from the date of service. If the work we did fails within 90 days, we return and correct it at no labor charge.

  2. 02

    Manufacturer parts coverage

    After the 90-day labor period, parts continue to be covered under their manufacturer warranties (Liftmaster, spring and door manufacturer terms). A $49.95 service fee for trip and diagnosis applies on out-of-labor-warranty calls.

  3. 03

    $200 labor cap on out-of-warranty replacements

    For opener replacements and spring replacements outside the 90-day labor period, our labor charge is capped at $200. Parts charged at standard rates.

  4. 04

    What's not covered

    The warranty does not cover: damage from misuse, vehicle impact, weather events, attempted self-repair, or normal wear on consumable parts (rollers, hinges, weatherstrip, light bulbs).

  5. 05

    How to make a warranty claim

    Call (916) 435-6320 with your service date and a description of the issue. We answer the phone Mon–Sat 7AM–7PM. Most warranty calls are scheduled within 1–2 business days.

  6. 06

    Licensed, bonded & insured

    Quality Garage Doors operates under California Contractor License #1042651, fully bonded and insured for residential garage door work across Sacramento, Placer and El Dorado Counties.
