Our Story

Sacramento's Garage Door Experts for Over 20 Years

Quality Garage Doors is a locally owned and operated business proudly serving the Greater Sacramento Region and surrounding areas including Placer and El Dorado Counties.

We offer same-day service, as well as 24/7 emergency service. We pride ourselves in our promptness — you can always expect a technician to arrive during the allotted time you were given.

We take pride in using only the highest quality parts to provide long-lasting repairs. With over a decade in the garage door industry, we've seen just about every garage door and opener, and every type of repair possible. It's because of this knowledge that we're able to offer such excellent service.

We offer repairs and service on all brands and models, keeping our customers happy and satisfied. We're licensed, bonded, and insured to take on any residential garage door job.
