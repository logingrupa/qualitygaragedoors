Know the Signs

When Is It Time for a New Door?

We always try to repair your existing door first. But sometimes the damage is too severe for repair. Here's what to look for:

Torn Panels

When tears have made it through the inside lip of the panel(s) and are visible on the front, it's time to replace.

Discontinued Manufacturer

If the manufacturer has gone out of business, replacement panels may no longer be available.

Structural Damage

Buckling, collapsing, or severe vehicle impact damage often means a full replacement is more cost-effective.
