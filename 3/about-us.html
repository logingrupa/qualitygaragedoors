About Quality Garage Doors

Our Story

We answer the phone. We show up. We fix it right.

Quality Garage Doors is a locally owned Sacramento garage door company built on a simple promise: honest pricing, same-day service, and work that holds up. We've spent over two decades fixing doors across Sacramento, Placer, and El Dorado Counties — and our reputation is the only thing we sell harder than our service.

Most calls we get are from homeowners with a car stuck inside and a busy day ahead. We get it. That's why we run lean, stock every truck for one-trip repairs, and quote flat prices before any wrench turns. No "while we're here" upsells. No bait-and-switch.
