FAQ

Quick Questions, Quick Answers

How fast can you get here?

Most calls before noon get a same-day visit. Mornings book up first — call early.

Why both springs at once?

Springs wear at the same rate. Replacing only the broken one over-tensions the new one and shortens its life. We always replace as a pair.

What's the free lube + tune?

It's a $129 standalone service: lube every moving part, tighten hardware, balance the door, test safety reverse. We include it free with every repair.

Do you charge a service fee?

$49.95 trip + diagnosis fee applies if you decline the repair. The fee is waived when you book the repair with us.

Is the work warrantied?

Yes — 90-day labor warranty on every repair. Parts continue under manufacturer terms after.
