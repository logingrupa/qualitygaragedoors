Warranty Information

Our Warranty, Plain & Simple

We stand behind every job. Here's exactly what's covered, for how long, and what happens after.

90 Days
Labor warranty on every repair and installation we perform.
Parts Coverage
Manufacturer parts warranty continues after the labor period ends.

What's Covered

90-Day Labor Warranty

If a part we installed fails within 90 days of service, we return and fix it at no additional labor cost. Just call us.

Parts-Only Coverage After 90 Days

After the 90-day labor period, parts remain covered under their manufacturer warranty. A $49.95 service fee applies for the trip and diagnosis.

Labor Cap on Opener & Spring Replacements

Out-of-warranty labor for opener or spring replacement is capped at $200, so you always know the maximum exposure up front.

Warranty Service Hours

Warranty service is scheduled Monday–Friday, 9AM–5PM. Need help outside those hours? Our standard same-day and 24/7 emergency service is always available.

Residential Garage Doors Only

We service residential garage doors. We do not service commercial roll-up doors or industrial systems.

Fine print: Warranty applies to parts and labor performed by Quality Garage Doors. It does not cover damage caused by misuse, accidents, weather events, attempted self-repair, or normal wear on consumables. Manufacturer warranties are passed through and governed by their own terms.
